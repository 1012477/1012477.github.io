Welcome to the Department of Defence

Who we are and what we do

The Department of Defence is responsible for looking after the safety of Australia and providing aid to countries and citizens in need. Australia's defence policy is founded on the principle of self-reliance in the direct defence of Australia, but with a capacity to do more where there are shared interests with partners and allies.
